Frequently Asked Questions

How do I plan a road trip route?
Open the app and tap the route planner. Add your start point, destination, and any stops along the way. Road Travel will suggest the best routes and show estimated drive times.
Does the app work offline?
Yes — you can download maps for offline use before your trip. Go to Settings → Offline Maps and select the regions you need.
How do I delete my account and data?
Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This will permanently remove your profile and all associated data. If you need help, contact us below.
I found a bug — how do I report it?
Use the contact option below and describe what happened, which device you're on, and the steps to reproduce the issue. Screenshots are always helpful!
