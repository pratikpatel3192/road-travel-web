Frequently Asked Questions

How does the weather-along-the-route feature work?
Road Travel samples points along your route and shows the forecast for the time you're expected to reach each one, so you see the conditions you'll actually drive through — not just the weather right now. Forecasts come from Apple Weather.
Why does the app ask for my location?
Location sets your starting point, powers turn-by-turn navigation, and lets the app check the weather ahead of you while you drive. You can change this anytime in Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services.
What is Road Travel Pro?
Pro is an optional subscription that unlocks unlimited routes, future departure planning, unlimited saved trips, and drive recording with driving stats.
How do I manage or cancel my subscription?
If you subscribed on your iPhone, billing goes through your Apple ID — manage or cancel in Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions. If you subscribed on the web, billing goes through Stripe — manage or cancel from Settings → Manage subscription at app.roadtravel.info. Deleting the app does not cancel a subscription.
How do I record a drive?
Drive recording is a Pro feature. Flip on Record this drive before you start navigating (iPhone); the app asks for your consent the first time. Your drive's route, distance, time, and stats are saved to your account — private by default. Delete any drive whenever you like; your stats update automatically.
How do friends work?
Add a friend by email from the Friends screen (iPhone) or the Driving page (web). They get an invitation email and a request in the app; requests expire after 7 days. Once accepted, a friend sees only the drives you explicitly mark Share with friends. You can unfriend or block anyone at any time — blocking hides you from them entirely.
How does chat work?
Message any accepted friend (or start a small group) from the Friends screen (iPhone) or the Chats page (web). You can share one of your recorded drives into a chat as a card — tapping it shows the route. Report any message from its long-press menu (iPhone) or the report link (web); blocking or unfriending someone closes your conversation with them.
How do I restore a purchase on a new device?
Open Road Travel, go to the Pro/paywall screen, and tap Restore. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to subscribe.
Is my data private?
Yes. Your trips, drives, and settings are stored in your account so they sync across devices, and everything social is opt-in and private by default. We don't show ads, don't track you across other apps, and never sell your data. You can export or delete everything from Settings. See our Privacy Policy.
