Frequently Asked Questions

How does the weather-along-the-route feature work?
Road Travel samples points along your route and shows the forecast for the time you're expected to reach each one, so you see the conditions you'll actually drive through — not just the weather right now. Forecasts come from Apple Weather.
Why does the app ask for my location?
Location sets your starting point, powers turn-by-turn navigation, and lets the app check the weather ahead of you while you drive. You can change this anytime in Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services.
What is Road Travel Pro?
Pro is an optional subscription that unlocks unlimited routes, future departure planning, and unlimited saved trips.
How do I manage or cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are billed through your Apple ID. Manage or cancel anytime in Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions on your iPhone. Deleting the app does not cancel a subscription.
How do I restore a purchase on a new device?
Open Road Travel, go to the Pro/paywall screen, and tap Restore. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to subscribe.
Is my data private?
Yes. Your trips and settings stay on your device, there are no accounts, and we don't track you. See our Privacy Policy.
