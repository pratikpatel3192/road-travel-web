Road Travel — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 1, 2026

Road Travel is a navigation and weather app that plans driving routes and shows the weather you'll meet along the way, time-shifted to your estimated arrival at each point. This policy explains what the app does with your information. We designed Road Travel to run on your device and to collect as little as possible.

Summary

  • Road Travel does not require an account and does not ask for your name or email.
  • Your trips, searches, and settings are stored on your device. We do not upload them to a server we control.
  • We do not track you across apps or websites, and we do not sell your data.
  • Location, maps, weather, and subscriptions are handled by Apple frameworks and one subscription provider (RevenueCat), described below.

Information the app uses

Location

With your permission, Road Travel uses your device location to set your current location as the starting point of a trip, provide turn-by-turn navigation, and check the weather ahead along your route (including while the screen is off, during active navigation). Location is processed on your device to compute routes and match forecasts. Road Travel does not store a history of your location on any server we operate. You can revoke location access at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services.

Routes, places, and saved trips

Origins, destinations, autocomplete searches, recent trips, and saved trips are stored locally on your device. They stay on your device unless you delete them or delete the app. Address search and routing are performed through Apple MapKit.

Weather

Weather forecasts are provided by Apple Weather (WeatherKit). When the app requests a forecast for points along your route, the relevant coordinates are sent to Apple to return the forecast. Apple's handling of that data is governed by Apple's privacy policy and the Apple Weather / WeatherKit terms. See Apple's data-source attribution at weatherkit.apple.com/legal-attribution.html.

Subscriptions (Road Travel Pro)

Optional subscriptions are sold through Apple's App Store (StoreKit) and managed with the help of RevenueCat, a subscription-infrastructure provider. To validate your purchase and keep your Pro status in sync across your devices, a random, app-generated identifier and your purchase/receipt information are processed by Apple and RevenueCat. This does not include your name, email, or Apple ID. Apple handles all payment information; Road Travel never sees or stores your payment details.

  • RevenueCat privacy policy: revenuecat.com/privacy
  • Apple's subscription and payment handling is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

Diagnostics

Any in-app event logging is used only to help the app function and is not used to build a profile of you or to track you across other apps or websites.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not require or collect accounts, names, or email addresses.
  • We do not use third-party advertising or ad tracking.
  • We do not sell or rent personal information.
  • We do not maintain a server-side history of your trips or locations.

Data retention and deletion

Because your trips and settings live on your device, you control them: delete individual saved trips in the app, or delete the app to remove its local data. Subscription records held by Apple and RevenueCat are retained per their respective policies.

Children

Road Travel is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected here with a new effective date.
